Accessible Text Flow

This is a readable step-by-step version of the same chart.

  1. Creators, artists, and collaborators can apply or join the Certifyd Network.
  2. After onboarding, a Certifyd ID is issued and creators publish or attach content.
  3. Creator publishing routes into either the Vassal Benford Content Network or the B.B. King Content Network.
  4. Fans and buyers choose content to unlock: premium media, access, and events.
  5. The choice routes into either the Vassal Benford Content Network or the B.B. King Content Network.
  6. Those networks publish through their web surfaces and connect to The Benford Company Certifyd Node.
  7. The node applies a node service fee and creates a Lightning invoice.
  8. Once payment is confirmed, content unlock is granted instantly.
  9. At the same time, split policy is enforced for settlement.
  10. Payout execution runs from split results.
  11. Certifyd audit trail records the transaction outcome.

“The Benford Company Network supports both sides of the content economy. Fans enter to unlock premium content. Creators enter to publish, collaborate, and monetize through Certifyd IDs. The Benford Company Certifyd Node provides the shared Bitcoin and Lightning commerce layer.”
