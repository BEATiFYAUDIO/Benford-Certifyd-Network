Fans enter through content unlock and choose among Vassal Benford, B.B. King, or other creator-owned content networks. Each network connects to its own web surfaces and can optionally connect to The Benford Company Certifyd Node for commerce services. The node handles Lightning checkout, invoicing, split policy enforcement, payouts, and audit records.

Accessible Text Flow

  1. Fans and buyers enter the Certifyd Network by choosing content to unlock: premium media, access, or events.
  2. They access content through a Content Network such as:
    • the Vassal Benford Content Network
    • the B.B. King Content Network
    • or other creator-owned Content Networks
  3. Creators, artists, and collaborators operate their own Content Networks.
  4. Each creator maintains their own identity and publishes content through their own Certifyd ID and web surfaces.
  5. Creators may choose to connect their Content Network to The Benford Company Certifyd Node for commerce services.
  6. The Benford Company Certifyd Node acts as a service provider. It offers Lightning infrastructure, checkout, invoicing, and split policy enforcement.
  7. The Benford Company Node Service Fee is applied at the service layer.
  8. When a purchase is made, the node creates a Lightning invoice.
  9. Once payment is confirmed, the buyer receives instant content unlock.
  10. At the same time, the defined split policy is enforced for settlement.
  11. Payout execution runs from the split results.
  12. The Certifyd audit trail records the transaction outcome.
  13. In the case of B.B. King content, rights and participation are controlled by the estate. Creators may participate only where authorized.
  14. The system allows independent creator networks to operate freely, while optionally using shared commerce infrastructure.

“The Benford Company Network supports both sides of the content economy. Fans enter to unlock premium content. Creators enter to publish, collaborate, and monetize through Certifyd IDs. The Benford Company Certifyd Node provides the shared Bitcoin and Lightning commerce layer.”
